The temporal lobe consists of structures that are vital for declarative or long-term memory.

What's on your mind?

This brain is constructed with Blender and rendered with Three.js. The model was exported with the glft exporter and all the lights, camera, and materials are setup with three.js. It uses a cube map for reflections and a directional light for the highlights. There's an ambient light for the fill and secondary colors.

My purpose in this peice was to revisit my roots in 3D animation and see what I could create utilizing WebGL.

Tools Used:

  • Blender
  • Three.js
  • Javascript
